Frequently Asked Questions

To start speech recognition, click the "Start" button. To stop, click the "Stop" button. The recognized text will be displayed in the textarea and saved to your history when you stop the recognition.

Click the copy icon button next to the text area. A message will appear at the bottom of the screen indicating that the text has been copied to the clipboard.

Click the "Clear History" button in the History section. This will remove all stored entries from your local storage and update the displayed history list.

Ensure that your browser supports the Web Speech API (e.g., Google Chrome). Check your microphone settings and permissions. If an error occurs, it will be displayed in the status message above the textarea.
